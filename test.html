How it Works

1. Search Pawsible to find "The One"

Take some time to read the profile of each pet, get to know their personality and find out if they are the right fit for you and your lifestyle. You’ll need to consider things such as size, age, activity levels, grooming requirements and training needs.

2. Get to know our rescue

If you’re new to rescue, it’ll help to learn a little about us. It’s important to build trust and ensure that you can feel a connection with your rescue group.

3. Let us know you're interested

Make your application. This gives us all the info we need to help you find the pet that’s right for you and your home. It may be that the pet you’re interested in has been adopted, so with the information you provide the rescue group may be able to suggest another suitable pet.

4. Meet and Greet

Once your application has been processed, the rescue group will contact you to arrange a time for you and your family to meet the pet. We may also request a house check, just to make sure your fences are escape-proof and there are no potentially dangerous situations your new pet may get into.
