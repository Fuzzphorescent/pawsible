How The Adoption Process Works

  1. Fill out an Application: If you find a pet you're interested in, fill out an adoption application. This application helps the organization assess whether you're a suitable match for the pet's needs.

  2. Interview: Some organizations may conduct an interview to understand your living situation, lifestyle, experience with pets, and why you want to adopt.
    Screening: Organizations may perform background checks, contact references, and verify information to ensure responsible pet ownership.

  3. Home Visit: Some organizations may conduct a home visit to ensure that your living environment is suitable and safe for the pet.

  4. Approval: Once your application is approved, you'll receive confirmation that you can adopt the pet. This may include details about the adoption fee, if applicable.
