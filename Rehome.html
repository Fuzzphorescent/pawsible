How To Help


1. Assessment and Care:
If you encounter an abandoned or stray animal, assess their condition. Check for injuries, malnutrition, or any immediate medical needs.
2. Safety and Identification:
If the animal is approachable, try to safely capture and contain it. Use caution, especially if the animal is frightened or injured.
3. Medical Care:
Discuss spaying or neutering options to prevent overpopulation and improve the animal's long-term health.
4. Stay Involved:
Your involvement can help ensure the animal's continued well-being.
